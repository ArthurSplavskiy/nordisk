PRIVACY POLICY

By using FreightCenter’s website (s) and quote system, you are permitting us to contact you regarding our freight services. The customer agrees to our PRIVACY POLICY that by establishing an account with FreightCenter, they have elected to opt-in to mailing and marketing communications from FreightCenter only. This communication includes service information, updates, savings offers, online advertising, and quarterly newsletters.

FreightCenter collects certain types of information when interacting with our websites, emails, and online ads. We use this secure data to improve how our website works, evaluate usability, capture website analytics, and support marketing campaigns. Our website uses Google Adwords and other online advertising platforms to advertise to new and previous visitors on third-party websites (including Google). An advertisement can be visible in search results or on a site in the Google Display Network (including other networks) based on visitor activity. Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on past visits to FreightCenter.com. We do not use, share or associate personally identifiable information with remarketing lists, cookies, or other anonymous information. To opt-out of interest-based ads, visitors can change their preferences by visiting Google’s Ads Settings.

When booking a shipment with FreightCenter, your personal contact information is used for confirmation, updates, and other pertinent information related to that particular booking. Our site does not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third-party firm.

Customers can elect to receive only specific communications by notifying us to change their email preferences. If you wish to modify your subscription, remove your name from our contact/mailing list, or have any questions, please contact us at privacy@freightcenter.com.
